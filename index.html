The Birthday Chronicle

Being a true and faithful account of the surprises awaiting Redhead · Compiled with the utmost discretion by a devoted friend

This author has it on the very best authority that today — the day of Her arrival into this world, all those years ago — has been arranged with a small procession of dispatches. Five, to be precise. Each sealed. Each timed. Each utterly undeserved by anyone but her.
